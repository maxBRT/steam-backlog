# Target user & positioning

Decisions from a grilling session (MXB-23).

## Headline

**Your Steam library is a mess. That's fine.**

*Keep playing what you love. Chip away at the rest when you feel like it.*

## Tagline

*Play what you love. Slowly make sense of the rest.*

## Who this is for

PC gamers whose Steam library is bigger than their appetite for games. Lots of bundle purchases, sale impulse buys, and games they'll probably never touch. They scroll their library and feel nothing. They want to keep playing without the pile weighing on them.

Cleaning a bloated library takes time. Steam Backlog is built for the long game: play now, organize slowly, no shame.

## Who this is not for

- **Multi-platform gamers** who need Epic, GOG, and Xbox in one launcher (Playnite territory)
- **Completionists and trackers** who want ratings, percentages, and social profiles (Backloggd territory)
- **Power organizers** who want tags, filters, and spreadsheet-level metadata

## How it works

1. **Board** (home) — A kanban of games you actually want to play: Queue, Up Next, Playing, Done.
2. **Triage** (side quest) — When you feel like it, review unreviewed games one at a time: Hide, Maybe, or Backlog.
3. **No guilt** — No streaks, no nagging, no finish line. Skip days. Leave games unreviewed. That's fine.

The app opens on Board. Triage is there when you're ready, not when the app demands it.

## What "done" looks like

- A short Board you trust: games you genuinely want to play.
- The rest stops weighing on you, even if you never review every game.

There is no inbox zero. Success is feeling in control, not finishing triage.

## Why us vs alternatives

| Alternative | Their job | Our job |
|---|---|---|
| **Steam Collections** | Sort games into folders | Give you a workflow: triage to decide, kanban to play |
| **Backloggd** | Log and rate what you played | Decide what you want to play next |
| **Playnite** | Launch games from every store | Organize intent, not files |

We are Steam-only by design. That is a scope choice, not a missing feature.

## Product posture

| Decision | Choice |
|---|---|
| Primary user | Overwhelmed accumulator (bloated library, bundle guilt) |
| Emotional contract | Relax. Marathon, not sprint. |
| Default screen | Board (play first) |
| Triage | Opt-in side quest |
| Guilt mechanics | None (no streaks, no "N unreviewed" nags) |
